This pack contains:

Seasonal forecast indicators for regional extremes, computed based on C3S-SEAS ensemble forecast
Computation is done for each member (51), and ensemble.

- heat waves (intensity, frequency, persistence);
- extreme instability indicators: K, TTI, CTI, VTI (>1.5 stdev; >1.75 stdev);
- scorching index (sum of Temperatures > 32C in JJA & number of days) ; 
- SPEI seasonal forecast (blending with high-resolution observations 1 km);
- Indicator_freezing_rain_favorable_conditions;
- Indicator_winter_storm (viscol);
- Regional_Indicator_Blizzard_conditions (based NAO and sst dipole in Mediterranean);
- Regional_RO_UE_indicator_Storm_track_frequency;
- Regional_Indicator_Flood_conditions.
